Dumbbell Shoulder Press

An absolute core exercise for the shoulder. But, I prefer using a machine rather than freeweight in terms of building muscle. This is because you have to balance the dumbbells whereas on the machine, all you have to do is push some weight. Now, I still occasionally do this exercise, and I used to do it more often until one time I lost balance of a dumbbell and thankfully did not have a freak accident. I kicked up the left dumbbell a little too hard and it just made my arm rotate a little further back than I would've liked. Right after I almost dislocated my shoulder, I continued to do shoulder presses like nothing happened. But overall, this is still a fantastic exercise that is just a little risky compared to its alternatives.
